## Trademark Usage Rights

### Proper Trademark Usage

**Always Use:**
- Surplusx™ (in marketing and formal documents)
- "The Surplusx brand" (when referring to brand identity)
- "Surplusx platform" (describing our service)
- Proper attributive adjective form

### Incorrect Usage (Avoid)

**Never Use:**
- ~~surplusx~~ (capitalization required in proper usage)
- ~~Surplusx~~ alone in legal documents (should include™)
- ~~Surplusx's~~ (possessive form discouraged in branding)
- As generic noun or verb
- Without proper attribution

### Trademark Notice Format

**Preferred Notice:**
```
Surplusx is a trademark of Sillionona.
© 2025-2026 Sillionona. All rights reserved.
```

**Alternative Notice:**
```
"Surplusx" is a registered trademark of Sillionona, Inc.
```
